RealTime and KIC will remain seperate study associations

Every now and then we hear people whispering that RealTime and KIC should merge for the 'better' good. A ridiculous idea, since both associations have their own identity, members, stories, events and a lot more!

RealTime and KIC will never merge. RealTime will never become a study section of KIC.

Why we don't merge

Our majors

RealTime is the study association for HBO-ICT, whereas KIC is the study association for IC/CO and CMD.

Office as a home

Our offices are places to work and to study. One office cannot simply suffice, since not everyone wants to study at the office.

Partners

We want to bring students in touch with the proper companies that suit the association in the best way.

General Assemblies

The board is never alone. Along with our members, we define what RealTime and KIC are for the present and the future.

"Merging would be stupid!"
