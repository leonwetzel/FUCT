A foundation for students of Communication, Media & IT

Students deserve the best life experience during their time at Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen. That's why the Forum for Unified Communication Technology will be there for all students of the School of Communication, Media and IT at Hanze UAS!

RealTime and KIC will combine forces and, as of the 1st of September 2017, merge and rebrand into the Forum for Unified Communication Technology. The associations will be terminated and the memberships of members of S.V. RealTime and S.V. KIC, including study sections GLiTCH and ICU, will end as of the 1st of September.

Why we merge

One to Many

We will connect all students at the School of Communication, Media & IT.

One office

Don't get lost! There will be one office for all students to ask all their questions.

Bigger events

More events for all majors. LAN party's, workshops, monthly drinks and a lot more!

No more General Assemblies

Memberships no longer exist! The board can now focus on organising great things for all students.

More experience

All students can now benefit from RealTime's seventeen years of experience and professionality.

More things together

Study sections will be terminated and all students can benefit from each other's experiences and knowledge.

A better international environment

Thanks to KIC's experiences, we can aid in integrating international students in Groningen's wonderful student life.

The best partners

Students are able to get in touch with the best companies and other organisations from Stad & Ommeland, and far beyond.

Merging means fitting. That will be our top priority!
